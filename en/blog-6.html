Pictory pricing in 2026: Starter vs Professional vs Team

Export minutes, AI Credits, ElevenLabs, Brand Kits: what to understand to choose the right plan without overpaying.

Published on April 9, 2026 Updated on April 9, 2026 ⏱️ 10 min read

Before you compare: 3 concepts that change everything

Pricing is not just the sticker price. To pick confidently, you must understand how Pictory measures usage:

  • Video minutes: the total exported duration for the month. Four 10-minute exports = 40 minutes used.
  • Per-project deduction: the idea is to deduct minutes on the first download of a project, then let you iterate (subsequent downloads of the same project) without charging every time.
  • AI Credits: a credit “wallet” for generative features (images, video, avatars). Credits reset monthly and do not roll over.

To check up-to-date numbers (they can change), refer to the official page: Pictory Pricing (checked on April 9, 2026).

2026 comparison: Starter, Professional, Team (real-world view)

Here is the “on-the-ground” reading of the three main plans: what you get and who it is for.

  • 60 min ElevenLabs (29 languages)

Great to start fast, publish consistently, and keep things simple.

For agencies and marketing teams producing at scale.

Which plan for long-form YouTube?

If you publish one 10-minute video per week, you export roughly 40 minutes/month. At that pace, Starter can work. Professional becomes worth it mainly if you:

  • repurpose multiple formats (long + shorts + variants)
  • need more Brand Kits (clients / brands)
  • use more ElevenLabs and/or AI features

Which plan for a team or agency?

Team becomes attractive as soon as you need to:

  • share assets and collaborate on the same projects
  • standardize branding/templates across multiple brands
  • export in volume without watching every minute

Tip: before upgrading, measure 30 days of usage (export minutes + AI credits).

AI Credits: included… then add-ons if needed

AI Credits can become the real bottleneck if you rely on avatars or generative video. The good news: you can purchase monthly add-on packs. Based on the official documentation, typical tiers are:

  • 500 credits: $25/month
  • 1000 credits: $49/month
  • 1500 credits: $70/month
  • 3000 credits: $135/month
  • 5000 credits: $199/month

To understand usage (images, video, avatars) and avoid burning credits, read: Pictory AI Credits (2026 guide).

FAQ

Do unused video minutes roll over?

Typically the system resets monthly (no rollover). Plan exports across the month if you produce in batches.

Is Starter enough for Shorts?

Yes if you export lightly (shorts use fewer minutes). AI Credits can still grow quickly if you generate a lot of visuals/avatars.

Monthly vs annual: which should I choose?

If you produce consistently, annual reduces the effective monthly cost. If your production is occasional, monthly keeps flexibility.

Should I use both Pictory and another editor?

Many creators do: draft and produce quickly in Pictory, then fine-tune captions, cuts, or effects in a dedicated editor.

Conclusion: pick based on usage, not price

In 2026, the right Pictory plan depends on export volume (minutes), branding needs (Brand Kits), and generative usage (AI Credits). Measure 30 days first, then upgrade only if necessary.
